Description

Match-3 meets roguelike! In Chainward, embark on a thrilling journey where you'll defeat monsters, face epic bosses, explore diverse locations, and perfect your deck of items and relics to create powerful synergies. Are you ready to forge your destiny, one chain at a time?

Features

  • Strategic Match-3 Gameplay
    Connect items in chains to unleash powerful effects. But think carefully—your matches are the key to survival.
  • Roguelike Progression
    Start your journey with a simple pack of items and forge your path through randomly picked locations filled with danger and opportunity.
  • Powerful Relics and Synergies
    Collect unique relics that amplify your abilities and open up new strategies. Build devastating combos and create synergies that suit your playstyle.
  • Dynamic Locations
    Each stop on your journey presents choices. Will you risk it all in a monster cave, relax at a tavern, or maybe do a little donation to the church?
